Breadboards

We connect battery or any other energy source to breadboard, and electricity flows through 2 first and last columns. There are many rows, called rails.

When we use resistor or wire to give electricity to one hole in rail, the whole rail is having acces to this electricity.

LEDs: use of LED should be done only with resistor in the same circuit to prevent the burn out. Longer leg is for positive charge and shorter - for negative.

Important notes: do not plug LED or resistor both ends in the same rail
