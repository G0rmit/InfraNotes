IC

IC - Integrated circuits. It has 14 legs, 2 for power(7th is for negative and 14th for positive) and groups of 3: 2 input and 1 output.

We start count of legs by placing the side with a small notch on the top. The first leg will be in the top left and the last one will be in te top right.

There are different versions of ICs. Some have AND gates, others, for example - XOR.
